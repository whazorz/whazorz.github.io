About me

Hi there! My name is Edmunds and I am a graphic designer from Latvia with over 10 years of experience. People know me by my nickname "Whazorz". Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to work on a wide range of projects for various clients, including advertisements, packaging, websites, and more. My expertise lies in graphic design, but I have a strong foundation in all areas of design and am proficient in various software programs. I am constantly learning and staying up-to-date with the latest design trends and technologies. My portfolio showcases a diverse range of work that demonstrates my skills and abilities. I am excited to bring my experience and creativity to new challenges and help my clients bring their vision to life. Thank you for considering me for your design needs!
